# Flipper RF literacy (curriculum)

1. **ISM bands** — what 315/433/868/915 mean in the US/EU (high level).
2. **Listen first** — passive RX, pulse edges are not messages.
3. **Safety gates** — edu_ack, confirm tokens, blacklists, cooldowns.
4. **USB discipline** — one serial owner; settle between bands.
5. **Site personality** — your lab’s “radio weather” over days.
6. **TX ethics** — owned gear only; ack_file vs full encoder.
